The Loxley Experience

We Don't Offer Amenities.
We Offer Chapters.

Your stay at Loxley is a story with a beginning, a middle, and a moment you'll still be talking about five years from now.

Chapter One

The Crossing

Your first steps across the hundred-foot rope bridge. The ground drops away. The trees rise to meet you. Your hands grip the ropes. Your heart beats faster. And then, halfway across, something happens — you stop noticing the height and start noticing the silence.

Available to every guest. It's not optional — it's the way home.

Chapter Two

First Light

The canopy at sunrise isn't a view. It's a slow-motion event. The mist lifts off the valley. The first birds call. The leaves above you glow gold, then green, then gold again. You're watching it from your bed, sixty feet in the air.

Available every morning the sun rises.

Chapter Three

Fire & Flour

Every guest finds fresh pizza dough waiting in their treehouse — our gift, your creation. Fire up the pizza oven on your sky deck, roll it out, build it however you want, and learn by doing. We include an appetizer-sized dough as your trial run — burn it, learn from it, laugh about it. Then make the real one. Somewhere between the first attempt and the last slice, you realize you're making pizza in the canopy of an ancient forest, and there's nowhere else you'd rather be.

Available exclusively for guests during their stay.

Chapter Four

After Dark

When the leaves drop and the canopy opens — November through early spring — the night sky from your deck is staggering. No light pollution. The Milky Way isn't something you look up at — it's something you're inside. In the warmer months, the canopy thickens and the stars peek through in pockets between the branches — a different kind of beautiful, like watching the sky through a cathedral window.

Available exclusively for guests during their stay.

Chapter Five

Fireside Cooking

Forty-two acres of hardwood cove forest. Trails through poplar, white oak, and mountain laurel. A creek that's been cutting this valley since before anyone remembers. Walk it slowly. There's no destination.

Available anytime. Shoes recommended. Agenda not.

The Morning Ritual

TreeMail

A small mailbox at the entrance to your rope bridge. Every morning, something appears inside. We won't spoil it — but you'll want to check before your first cup of coffee.

Part of every stay.

Every Evening

Stars & S'mores

Graham crackers, chocolate, marshmallows, and a fire you built yourself. The stars do the rest.

Part of every Loxley stay.

The Hidden Chapter

The Letter

On the desk you'll find stationery, a pen, and an envelope. Write a letter — to yourself, to your partner, to the person you want to be a year from now. Seal it. Leave it with us. In twelve months, it arrives at your door.

Available to every guest.
