Elopements at Loxley Forest

Your Aisle Is a Hundred Feet Long
and Sixty Feet in the Air

No ballrooms. No banquet chairs. No receiving line. Just two people, an ancient forest, and a ceremony as dramatic as the love that brought you here.

Inquire About Elopement Packages

Most elopement venues give you a pretty backdrop. We give you a transformation.

Your ceremony begins on solid ground. You walk together toward the rope bridge — a hundred feet of suspended pathway over the forest floor, swaying gently between two ancient white oaks. Every step is a choice. Every step is toward each other. By the time you reach the other side, something has changed. The ordinary world is behind you. And you're standing in the canopy, sixty feet above the earth, in a place that was built for exactly this moment.

Say your vows on the deck of a treehouse that one man built with his hands. Exchange rings beside a fire pit set at the four compass points — a ritual space designed with the weight and intention of ceremonies that have existed for five thousand years. Let the forest witness it. Let the silence hold it.

Then stay the night. Wake up married in the sky.
